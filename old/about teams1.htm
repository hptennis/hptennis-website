About our Hampden Park Tennis Teams

Our aim at Hampden Park is to offer all players the opportunity to play competitive matches at an appropriate  level for their playing ability if they are keen to play. The year is split into Summer season and Winter, with  separate leagues organised for each. Home matches generally take place on Sundays. We currently run 4 men’s  teams playing in the Sussex league ranging in standard from Division 2 to Division 10 East. Our 3 women’s  teams compete in a wide range of standards with our 1st team playing at National level and the 3rd team  playing in Division 9 East of the Sussex league.   Each team is organised by a volunteer captain who attempts to select teams of appropriate playing standard to  be competitive in the division the team is placed in. The objective of the league system is to give the  opposition a close match and teams are promoted or relegated based on performance though the season.    Ultimately the teams are run for the enjoyment of players and to encourage this there is a social aspect in  that all matches finish with refreshments for both teams where a chat analysing the match can take place.  Anyone interested in playing team tennis would be warmly welcome and should contact -   Club Coach Jenny Hughes  at hughes_j32@sky.com  or   Richard Ellis at ellisfamily54@gmail.com  - Phone: 01323 644093
