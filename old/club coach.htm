Get to know Jenny - our Club Coach Jenny, our club coach, has been at the club since 1997. She runs a full coaching programme  catering for both adults and Juniors of all ages. She runs group and individual lessons  including Mini Tennis for 5 - 10 year olds and Cardio Tennis for anyone who wants to improve  their fitness in a fun tennis way!  Jenny regularly competes in the club’s Ladies 1st team in the Aegon National League, which  has been in the South East Regional Division for over 12 years!  Jenny competes in the Ladies 1st Team and has represented GB four times playing in  Majorca, San Diego, Palm Beach and, most recently, in France  She has played for England three times in the Four Nations Seniors event in 2005, 2010 and  2013. England won two of the events and were second in Dublin in 2013! Individually Jenny’s  best achievement was to win the National Seniors Grass Court Championships in the Ladies over 40’s singles in 2006. This was held  at the All England Club, Wimbledon, She has also won several National Doubles events.  She is a regular Sussex over 35’s and 40’s team member and was part of a string of great  successes as the 35’s team won the County Championship Group 1 for the years, 2011 - 2015 and  for the first time the Sussex 40’s team won the County Championships final to be County  Champs 2013! These teams were then awarded Team of the Year for Sussex 35’s in 2011 and  2012, and for the 40’s in 2013. In 2014, as a team member, she won the 35s Group 1 again for 4th year in a row and were  awarded Sussex Adult Outstanding Achievement Award.  She was also Runner up in the 50s singles at the Senior National Grass Court Champs held at the  All England club  Changing the subject, perhaps her greatest achievement was to complete the Brighton Marathon  in aid of Macmillan Cancer Support in 5hours and 17 minutes in 2012! Jenny raised a remarkable  £4,500 for Macmillan!
I think I’ll stick to tennis!
