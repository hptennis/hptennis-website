Welcome to Hampden Park Juniors Download March Annual Newsletter Hampden Park has a very active junior programme with approximately 80 members. Coaching sessions, including mini-tennis, are available with our licensed Senior Club Coach ; Jenny Hughes, TenniscoachUK National Member. Junior membership is available for  £39.00 per annum. Junior coaching squads These are available for all ages and standards on Saturday mornings, Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday evenings. Maximum 12 per session. Check with the club calender  for current times. Cost: £4 per hour per child. LTA Mini Tennis Award scheme This is the ideal way for young people to get started with the game and to develop through the  Red, Orange and Green levels. Select the "Mini tennis" link for more details. Junior Aces Sundays 6-8pm This is a weekly junior club night with a mixture of singles and doubles matches plus fun team games and on ongoing singles ladder. Cost £3.00 per child: Continues throughout the year. General activities Fun tournaments in school holidays. Teams in Sussex Junior Tennis leagues. Junior Aces - All year, Sunday matchplay sessions. Crash courses in Easter and summer holidays. Adult/junior tournaments Junior Club Championships - Singles and Doubles. Junior ladder competitions Junior of the year award. In brief, every opportunity is given for juniors of all levels of ability to develop a taste for the exciting sport of tennis. Contact the club coach Jenny Hughes (coach@hampdenparktennis.co.uk) or on 07831 097864 for further details.
