Coaching at Hampden Park
Individual coaching:  If you want to improve your game, contact Jenny Hughes (07831 097864) who will be pleased to organise private or group lessons. If you book 3 individual lessons, members will get the fourth one free. Cost per lesson: £25 per hour. Adult group coaching sessions are held on a Thursday night from 7.00 -8.00 p.m. at £5 per hour (£6 non-members). Anyone welcome to attend and dates are posted up on the clubhouse notice board. Cardio tennis on Monday evenings at 6.45 p.m. is a great fun weekly way to get fit while enjoying your tennis.      Open to all : members £5.00          non members £6.00 Junior Coaching. There is an extensive Junior Tennis coaching programme with coaching taking place throughout the week and at the week end. Extra coaching activities tale place during school holidays. See our “Junior Tennis" page for more details.
