Saturday 16 May 13:00 - 15:00 Come along to Hampden Park Tennis Club and try lots of  different Tennis activities for Free. All the family are  welcome and can play together at the same times. Any age and standard are  welcome to come and play! 

Ball Machine Session

Come and try to beat the Ball Machine! Hit lots of  balls at lots of targets! All the family welcome  Booking times  13:00 - 13:30  

Cardio Tennis

Cardio Tennis is a fun, sociable group fitness class set on a  tennis court and open to people of all ages and abilities.  Supported by motivating music and qualified instructors,  you’ll get to hit lots of tennis balls during your class and  have a great cardio workout at the same time.   Booking times  14.00 - 14:30

Fastest Serve Session

Your chance to prove you've got the fastest serve in the  family and of all your friends!   Booking times  13:30 - 14.00

Free Play

If you don’t fancy a coaching session why not just try out  our facilities. You can play between 13.00-15.00 hrs.   Booking times  13:00 - 15.00
